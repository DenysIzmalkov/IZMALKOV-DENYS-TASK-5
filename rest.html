Menu

Garden Fresh Salad

Crisp mixed greens topped with sliced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, shredded carrots, and red onion. Served with your choice of dressing on the side. Add grilled chicken or shrimp for an additional protein boost. Perfect as a starter or a light and healthy meal option.

Summer Harvest Salad

A refreshing blend of mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber slices, shaved carrots, and red onion, all tossed in a zesty citrus vinaigrette. Topped with a sprinkle of feta cheese and crunchy roasted almonds for added texture and flavor. Perfect for those hot summer days when you want something light, yet satisfying.

Mixed Green Salad

A fresh and vibrant mix of baby greens, including spinach, arugula, and romaine lettuce, tossed with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, sliced radishes, and toasted almonds. Finished with a zesty citrus vinaigrette dressing, this salad is the perfect light and refreshing meal for any time of the day. Optional add-ons include grilled chicken, shrimp, or tofu for added protein.
